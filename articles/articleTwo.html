Let's talk about the pause menu...

Hello! Welcome to today's blog post!

Today, we'll talk about the pause menu. The pause menu is something vital on a videogame, it allows you to go to eat when your mom calls you. You just gotta press the start or enter button to make the magic appear. In System32 - System Crashdown, the pause menu has been through a lot of redisigns, i'll talk you about all of them in this post.

The first design we cooked.

The first design we cooked was a very simple one, in fact, is the one used by default in Haxeflixel (the game engine we use lol). You just had to pause the game pressing enter, and to resume it, just press enter again. Im not gonna show screenshots of this one, because... why?

The second one.

The second design was pretty cool, it had cool animations, buttons and more stuff. And you will be asking yourself... why we discarded this one?

And the answer is... we trashed it bc i was bored and recided to "redesign" the pause menu.
