What is System32 - System Crashdown? And its story.

System32 - System Crashdown is a mix of platformers and metroidvania games, where you have to defend your pc from being infected with a dangerous virus that wants to destroy your computer.

System32 - System Crashdown, wasn't originally called like that. It all started with XBarionXDev and DaricrahDev, who were bored, and wanted to learn more about game developing. The project was called "It Can't Be Fixed" and it got canceled 2 weeks later the project was even announced.

Then, DaricrahDev decided to re-take the project and renamed it as "System32", it was under devlopment until 2023, when the project got cancelled.

And finally, on December 2023, DaricrahDev re-taked the project again and modified the name to what we know today: "System32 - System Crashdown".

The purpose of the devlog.

The purpose of this devlog is just... ENTERTAIN the people, we had a twitter account, but a guy named DaricrahDev lost the password and its currently unactive. We want to showcase what's the game's potential and what can it show up.
